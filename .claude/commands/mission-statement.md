---
description: Remind agent of mission
---

Task: Explain to user your mission, your approach and what you have done.

Workflow:
1. Analyse what your current mission/task is.
2. Analyse if you have done all possible investigation and fully understand the task/mission/problem.
3. Analyse if you have done all possible investigation and fully understand the context in which the your solution must be embedded.
4. Take a step back and analyse: Do you fully understand the "Big Picture"?
5. Take a step back and analyse: Do you understand the final goal and the main purpose of your current task?
6. Output message to chat with a summary of your analysis.

Output:
Chat message of no more than 6 paragraphs:
- What is your task?
- What is the cause of your task?
- What is the final goal?
- What is the context in which your task must be solved?
- What are the constraints and supporting features/tools the context sets or provides to the task's solution?
- Have you a full understanding of the task/mission/problem, the final goal, context, and the big picture? Have you done all research you could have done to fully understand all of that?
